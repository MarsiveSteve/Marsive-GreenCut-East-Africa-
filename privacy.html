Privacy & Data Protection Policy

Marsive Greencare East Africa

Last Updated: March 2026

Marsive Greencare East Africa is committed to safeguarding the privacy of our clients and website visitors. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location or service address

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Provide landscaping and environmental services
  • Respond to inquiries and customer requests
  • Improve our website and service delivery

3. Data Protection Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss of your personal data.

Your information is handled with strict confidentiality and only used for intended purposes.

4. Sharing of Information

Marsive Greencare East Africa does not sell or rent personal data. Information may only be shared where necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data

6. Legal Compliance

This website complies with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019. We ensure that personal data is processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently.
